connect port
	- Connect to EV3 device on port.
	- Ex.: connect COM4

disconnect
	- Disconnect from EV3 device.
	- Ex.: disconnect

beep volume frequency durationMs
	- Play tone with given volume, frequency and duration.
	- Ex.: beep 10 440 300

led state
	- Change the leds state on the brick.
	- Ex.: led greenflashing
